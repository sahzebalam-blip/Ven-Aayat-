Privacy Policy

This page outlines how information is collected, used and handled across website interactions and project communication.

Overview

Ven Aayat Tech Solutions handles submitted information with a focus on clarity, operational use and responsible communication. Information shared through this website is used to understand project requirements, respond to inquiries and support structured digital execution.

Information Collected

We may collect information submitted voluntarily through forms, email or direct communication, including name, email, project details, references and supporting materials. Basic technical data such as device type or browser information may also be collected for performance and stability.

Use of Information

Submitted data may be used to evaluate project scope, respond to inquiries, prepare direction or proposals and maintain communication related to services. Information is not collected for unrelated resale or spam activity.

Confidentiality

Project-related discussions are treated as business communication. Users should avoid sharing sensitive credentials such as passwords, private keys or restricted access information through standard contact forms.

Cookies and Technical Data

Basic cookies or analytics tools may be used to improve website performance and user experience. These do not automatically reveal personal identity unless explicitly provided.

Third-Party Services

Some services may involve external tools such as hosting providers, analytics platforms or communication services. These operate under their own policies and environments.

Data Retention

Information may be retained for communication, project tracking or operational purposes where necessary.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that shared information is appropriate for general communication channels. Sensitive or restricted data should not be submitted through public forms.

Updates

This policy may be updated over time to reflect changes in operations, structure or requirements.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, users may contact through the official email listed on the website.
